Global Expansion

Spatial Narratives
Across Borders

A chronological retrospective of our international interior decor installations. From private residential estates to diplomatic architectural spaces, our design footprint bridges regional heritage with modern minimalism.

01 // 2010 Residential Archive

Madrid, Spain

Our premier European architectural intervention. This interior project focused on harmonizing raw masonry with low-slung, custom-tailored oak features to capture natural Iberian daylight patterns.

Material focus Stoned Travertine, Smoked Oak
Spatial Concept Symmetric Restraint
02 // 2015 Coastal Villa

Mexico

An exploration into earth materiality. The design balances raw, heavily textured walls with smooth dark-stained elements to establish a deeply peaceful, grounding architectural landscape.

Material focus Waxed Concrete, Dark Bamboo
Spatial Concept Organic Fluidity
03 // 2018 Atelier Studio

Milan, Italy

Executed during the international design exposition. This historical apartment layout was stripped back to isolate negative space, showcasing custom furniture profiles against architectural white canvases.

Material focus Carrara Marble, Matte Lacquer
Spatial Concept Negative Volume
04 // 2022 Penthouse Suite

Dubai, UAE

A high-altitude layout designed around structural symmetry and panoramic views. Tailored bespoke furniture structures frame minimalist hearth zones built directly into primary limestone boundaries.

Material focus Limestone, Brushed Bronze
Spatial Concept Monolithic Scale
